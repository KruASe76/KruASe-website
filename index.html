My class schedule [RU]

The schedule of my class that changes too often brang to my mind to make a shedule that will be accessible from everywhere and up to date almost always in one place. So, I made this. The schedule that is easy to change because of dynamic filling with names of subjects that depends on table cells classes. On Wednesday my class is divided into two groups, and each of them has its own schedule. So, I added a button that swithes the group and edits the schedule, and selected status is saved and keeps even after browser closing.
